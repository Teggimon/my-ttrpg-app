Background
Outlander
Background Feature
Wanderer. You have an excellent memory for maps and geography and can always recall general terrain layouts. You can find food and fresh water for yourself and up to five others each day in natural environments.
Personality
Traits

Ideals
Greater Good. It is each person's responsibility to make the most happiness for the whole tribe. (Good)

Appearance
Physical Description
Tall and weathered, dark hair streaked grey at the temples. Grey eyes that miss little. A long scar runs from left jaw to collarbone. Wears a worn Ranger's cloak over battered but well-maintained armour.
Backstory
Character History
Born Aragorn II, son of Arathorn, raised in Rivendell under the name Estel — hope — kept ignorant of his true heritage until his twentieth year. He has spent decades ranging the wilds of Eriador, protecting settlements from orc raids, known to most only as Strider...
Allies & Organisations
Rangers of the North Organisation
A secretive brotherhood of Dúnedain Rangers who patrol Eriador. Aragorn serves as their Chieftain, though few outside the order know this.
Gandalf the Grey NPC · Ally
An Istari wizard of great power. A trusted advisor whose counsel is rarely wrong and never simple.
